Implicit Bias in Algorithms


What is implicit bias?

Implicit bias can be found in technology all around us,
from social media to crucial healthcare algorithms.
But what is implicit bias?

Implicit bias refers to the unconscious attitudes
or sterotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions.

What is an algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of instructions to solve a particular problem. Everything and everyone needs to these algorithms for everyday applications.

Without these algorithms, our everyday lives would be significantly more difficult. But what if we were to tell you that these algorithms are already failing to work for a large part of the population?

The reason for this is that algorithms themselves can have implicit bias. The companies that provide these algorithms to run our social media and even our healthcare systems are made up teams that don't reflect the populace. That is the problem our website is trying to address. We want to hold these companies accountable.
